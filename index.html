Roadmap of Course

Learn the core concepts, practical methods, and ethical considerations of journalism in this life-changing two-week online course.

DAY 1: Introduction to Journalism: History, Ethics, and Purpose
DAY 2: The Art of Interviewing: Techniques and Approaches

DAY 4: News Writing Essentials: Crafting Clear and Compelling Stories

DAY 12-14: Final Projects and Presentations: Applying Skills to Real-World Scenarios
